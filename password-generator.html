🔐 Free Password Generator

How to use the password generator

  1. Choose your desired password length using the slider (16+ characters is recommended for important accounts).
  2. Toggle uppercase, lowercase, numbers and symbols on or off based on the site's requirements.
  3. Click Generate Password. The result appears in the field above.
  4. Click Copy and paste it into the password field on the site you are signing up for.
  5. Store it in a reputable password manager so you don't need to remember it.

Why password strength matters

Weak or reused passwords are the leading cause of personal account compromise. Each extra character roughly multiplies the time an attacker needs to crack the password. A 16-character random password is exponentially stronger than an 8-character one — even one with symbols.

What makes a password strong?

  • Length first. Length beats complexity. Aim for 16+ characters.
  • Randomness. Avoid dictionary words, names, dates and keyboard patterns like qwerty.
  • Uniqueness. Never reuse a password across sites.

Is this generator safe?

Yes. Passwords are generated locally in your browser using the W3C crypto.getRandomValues API, which is cryptographically secure. Nothing is sent to or logged by our servers.

FAQ

How long should my password be? 12 characters is a minimum; 16+ is recommended; 20+ for financial or email accounts.

Should I include symbols? Yes, when the site allows them. Some legacy systems reject symbols — uncheck the box in that case.
