Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

ToolHub ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we DO NOT collect

  • We do not collect passwords, text, images, JSON, URLs or any other input you type into our tools. Tool inputs are processed entirely in your browser and never transmitted to our servers.
  • We do not require accounts, sign-ups, or email addresses to use any tool.
  • We do not sell, rent or trade personal information.

Information we DO collect

Server logs. Like most websites, our hosting provider (Cloudflare) automatically logs basic request metadata — IP address, user agent, requested page, timestamp — for security and abuse prevention. These logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days.

Cookies. ToolHub itself does not set tracking cookies. Third-party services we use (see below) may set their own cookies.

Third-party services

  • Google AdSense. We display ads served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies (including the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Cloudflare. We use Cloudflare for hosting and DDoS protection. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies.
  • QR Code API. The QR Code Generator sends the text you enter to api.qrserver.com to render the image. Do not enter sensitive data into this tool.
  • Google Fonts. Fonts are loaded from fonts.googleapis.com.

Your rights

Under GDPR and CCPA you have the right to access, correct or delete personal data we hold about you. Since we collect almost none, most requests are simple — contact us at privacy@toolhub.example.

Children's privacy

ToolHub is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.
