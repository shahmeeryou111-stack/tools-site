Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Lorem Ipsum come from?

It's a scrambled version of a text by Roman philosopher Cicero from 45 BC. Designers have used it since the 1500s.

Why use Lorem Ipsum instead of real text?

Placeholder Latin lets viewers focus on layout and typography without being distracted by content meaning.

Is the text really meaningful?

No — it's intentionally nonsensical so it doesn't distract from design review.

Can I use it in published work?

It's safe to use during design but should always be replaced with real content before publishing.
