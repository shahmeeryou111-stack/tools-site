📏 Free Unit Converter

How to use the unit converter

  1. Pick a category — length, weight, time or temperature.
  2. Enter the value you want to convert.
  3. Choose the source and target units — the result updates instantly.

Supported units

Length: millimeter, centimeter, meter, kilometer, inch, foot, yard, mile
Weight: gram, kilogram, ton, ounce, pound
Time: second, minute, hour, day, week
Temperature: Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin

Accuracy

All conversions use internationally standardised constants. Results are rounded to six decimal places — more than enough for everyday use.

Common conversions

  • 1 mile = 1.609 km
  • 1 inch = 2.54 cm
  • 1 pound = 0.453592 kg
  • 0 °C = 32 °F = 273.15 K
