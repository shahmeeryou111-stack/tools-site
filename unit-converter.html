Frequently Asked Questions

Is the converter accurate?

Yes — all conversion factors are based on international SI definitions, accurate to 6 decimal places.

How does temperature conversion work?

Internally all temperatures convert through Celsius using the standard formulas (F = C×9/5+32; K = C+273.15).

Can I convert currencies?

Not yet — currencies require live exchange rates. We focus on physical units.

What's the largest unit?

Length supports up to miles; weight up to metric tons; time up to weeks.
