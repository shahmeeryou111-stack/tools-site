🖼️ Free Image Compressor

How to use the image compressor

  1. Click Choose an image and select a JPG, PNG or WebP file.
  2. Adjust the quality slider — 0.7 is a good default that's nearly indistinguishable from the original.
  3. Pick an output format (WebP gives the best compression for most photos).
  4. Click Download compressed.

Lossy vs lossless compression

Lossy (JPEG, WebP) discards information your eye can't easily perceive, giving much smaller files. Lossless (PNG) preserves every pixel — better for graphics with sharp edges, screenshots and logos.

Which format to pick

  • JPEG — photos, complex images with smooth gradients
  • WebP — modern alternative, ~25–35% smaller than JPEG at the same quality
  • PNG — logos, line art, anything with transparency

Privacy

All compression happens in your browser via the HTML5 Canvas API. Your image is never uploaded.
