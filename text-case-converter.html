Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Title and Sentence case?

Title Case capitalizes Every Major Word. Sentence case only capitalizes the first word and proper nouns.

When should I use camelCase vs snake_case?

camelCase is standard in JavaScript and Java. snake_case is common in Python and SQL.

Will it preserve my line breaks?

Yes — line breaks and paragraph structure are preserved.

Does it work with non-English text?

Yes — Unicode is fully supported.
