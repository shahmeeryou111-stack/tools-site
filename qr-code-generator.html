Frequently Asked Questions

Do QR codes expire?

No. QR codes are static images — they never expire. As long as the URL they point to is alive, the code works forever.

Can I print these QR codes?

Yes. Use 300px or larger for print. Always test-scan before mass production.

Are these QR codes free to use commercially?

Yes — fully free for personal and commercial use, no attribution required.

What's the minimum scannable size?

Generally 2x2cm (0.8x0.8in) on print. Larger = easier scanning from a distance.
