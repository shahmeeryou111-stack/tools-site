📱 Free QR Code Generator

How to use the QR code generator

  1. Enter the URL or text you want to encode.
  2. Adjust the size if you need a larger or smaller image.
  3. Click Generate QR Code and download the PNG.

What can a QR code contain?

Any short string of text — URLs, contact cards (vCards), Wi-Fi credentials, phone numbers, plain text. For best scan reliability keep the encoded text under 300 characters.

Where to use QR codes

  • Business cards, flyers and posters
  • Restaurant menus
  • Product packaging
  • Event check-in
  • Wi-Fi sharing at home or office

Design tips

  • Print at a minimum size of 2×2 cm with at least 4 modules of quiet zone (white space) around the code.
  • Ensure strong contrast between dark and light modules.
  • Test the code with multiple phones before printing in bulk.
