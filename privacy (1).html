Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Overview

ToolHub ("we", "us") operates this website. This page explains what information we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have.

2. Data we do not collect

The tools on ToolHub run entirely in your browser. The text, files, passwords, JSON, URLs, and images you process with our tools are never transmitted to our servers, never logged, and never stored.

3. Data we do collect

  • Standard server logs — IP address, browser user-agent, referrer, and pages requested. These logs are kept for up to 30 days for security and abuse prevention.
  • Analytics — we may use a privacy-respecting analytics service to count page views in aggregate. No personal profile is built.

4. Cookies

ToolHub does not set its own tracking cookies. Third-party services we embed (advertising, analytics) may set their own cookies — see section 5.

5. Third-party advertising

We use third-party advertising companies, including Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

6. Children's privacy

ToolHub is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are in the EU, UK, or California, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. Contact privacy@toolhub.example.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@toolhub.example.
