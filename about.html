About ToolHub

Built for the open web — fast, private, free forever.

ToolHub is a hand-picked collection of 10 essential online tools for developers, designers and everyday creators. Every tool runs entirely in your browser — your data never leaves your device.

Our mission

Most online tool sites bury simple utilities under aggressive ads, sign-up walls and tracking scripts. We believe a password generator or JSON formatter should be one click away, load instantly and respect your privacy. ToolHub is our answer to that.

How we build

  • Client-side first. Whenever physically possible, tools process your input locally in JavaScript — no servers, no uploads.
  • Zero tracking. We don't run analytics, profilers or ad networks that follow you across the web.
  • Open patterns. Each tool is a single self-contained page with clean HTML and CSS. View source any time.
  • Performance. Every page is under 50 KB of HTML/CSS/JS and loads in under a second on a 3G connection.

Sustainability

We display a small number of unobtrusive ads to cover hosting costs. If you'd like to support ToolHub directly, the best thing you can do is share us with a friend.
