About ToolHub

ToolHub is a free collection of online utilities built for developers, designers, marketers and everyday internet users. Every tool is designed around three principles: speed, privacy, and simplicity.

Our mission

The web is full of tool sites that bury simple functions under ads, signup walls and unnecessary tracking. ToolHub takes the opposite approach — open the page, use the tool, leave. No friction.

How the tools work

Every tool on ToolHub runs entirely in your browser using standard web APIs. Your input never leaves your device. That makes the tools faster (no network round-trip) and safer (no data on our servers to leak).

Who builds ToolHub

ToolHub is built and maintained by a small independent team of web developers who use these tools every day in our own work. If a tool you need is missing, tell us.

How we make money

ToolHub is supported by unobtrusive display advertising. We do not sell user data, we do not require accounts, and we do not run sponsored or affiliate redirect links inside the tools. Ads are clearly labeled and never interfere with tool functionality.
